RATIONALE FOR DETERMINATION
Criterion RX-114.2 - Step Therapy Requirement Not Met
Policy RX-114.2 requires documented treatment with at least one
conventional synthetic DMARD for a minimum of three (3) consecutive
months with inadequate clinical response. The records submitted do
not document a completed qualifying DMARD trial of at least three
months' duration.
Criterion RX-114.3 - Disease Activity Not Documented
Policy RX-114.3 requires a validated disease activity measure
(DAS28-CRP, CDAI, or SDAI) obtained within ninety (90) days of the
request demonstrating moderate or high disease activity. No
qualifying disease activity score was identified in the submitted
documentation.
