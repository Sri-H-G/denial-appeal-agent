RATIONALE FOR DETERMINATION
Criterion RA-2(a) - Conventional DMARD Trial Not Documented
Program 2025 P 2198-11 requires documented history of failure to a
3-month trial of a non-biologic disease-modifying antirheumatic drug
(DMARD) (e.g., methotrexate, leflunomide, sulfasalazine,
hydroxychloroquine) at maximally indicated doses, unless
contraindicated or clinically significant adverse effects are
experienced. No qualifying conventional DMARD trial was identified
in the records submitted with this request.
Criterion RA-4 - Rheumatology Prescriber Not Confirmed

or in consultation with a rheumatologist. The submitted request did
not include documentation confirming the prescriber's specialty.
